💙 Martinez’s Flowers

🌼 My Story

I’ve always been the kind of person who jumps from hobby to hobby.

One week it’s drawing, the next it’s something completely different. I get excited easily, try a lot of things, and sometimes move on just as fast. For a while, I thought maybe I just hadn’t found my thing yet.

But then I discovered making pipe cleaner flowers.

It started simple just bending and twisting, figuring things out as I went. But something about it stuck. I didn’t get bored. I didn’t move on. I kept going.

And for the first time, I didn’t just want to make something for myself I wanted to share it with other people.

These flowers aren’t just crafts to me. They’re the first hobby that made me slow down, stay consistent, and actually feel proud of what I’m creating.

So this is me putting it out there.
